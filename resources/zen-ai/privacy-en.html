Privacy Policy

Zen AI is an independent desktop application based on the Cherry Studio open-source codebase. We aim to keep the app local-first and minimize data collection.

1. What Zen AI stores locally

  • App settings and local preferences
  • Conversations, files, notes, and knowledge-base data that you create in the app
  • Optional local cache, logs, and backup files needed for normal operation

2. What Zen AI does not do by default

  • No built-in analytics reporting
  • No built-in automatic update checks
  • No automatic upload of your local conversations or API keys to Zen AI-operated servers

3. Third-party AI providers and services

When you configure model providers, web services, cloud backup, OAuth login, or similar integrations, Zen AI will connect directly to those third-party services using the credentials and endpoints you provide.

Data sent to those services is governed by their own privacy policies and terms. Please review them before use.

4. Backups and exports

If you choose to export data or configure WebDAV, S3, Nutstore, or other backup destinations, your data will be transferred to the destination you selected. Zen AI does not provide a hosted backup service.

5. Open-source distribution

Zen AI is distributed as open-source software. Source releases are published at the project repository so users can inspect how the software works.
